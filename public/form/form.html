You can take only up to 3 Jet Ski at the same time. Jet Ski takes up to 2 persons on-board.

You can take up to 4 kayaks with maximum capacity of 2 persons at the same time.

You can take only up to 4 SUPs at the same time. Only one person can stay on each SUP.

You can take only up to 4 pedalos with maximum capacity of 4 persons at the same time.
